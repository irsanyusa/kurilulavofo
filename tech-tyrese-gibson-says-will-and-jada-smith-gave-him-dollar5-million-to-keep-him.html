Tyrese Gibson says Will and Jada Smith gave him $5 million to keep him 'afloat' during a bitter cust

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  261 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Tyrese Gibson thanked Jada Pinkett Smith and Will Smith for giving him $5 million in an Instagram post on Monday.

Gibson said the couple sent him the money to help keep him "afloat" and that they told him to "get off and stay off the internet" now that his "daughter's legal fees will be paid."

On Wednesday, Tyrese Gibson shared a video on Facebook pleading with her not to keep his daughter from him.

In the video, the "Fast and the Furious" star also brought up his financial woes, saying, "I'm at $13,000 a month — what more do you want from me?"

Court documents obtained by People in September show that full custody of Shayla Somer Gibson was temporarily granted to Norma Mitchell Gibson and that Tyrese Gibson was ordered to stay 100 yards away from his ex-wife, his daughter, and his ex-wife's home.

Norma Mitchell Gibson said that on August 19, Tyrese Gibson "pinned" his daughter's face down, "put his knees on her back, grabbed her hands with one arm, and beat her with the other," People reported, citing the court documents.
