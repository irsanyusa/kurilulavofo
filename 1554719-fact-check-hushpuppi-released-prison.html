Hushpuppi Finally Released From US Prison? Fact Emerges

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  392 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Nigerian Instagram celebrity, Ramon Abbas, known as “Hushpuppi” began serving an 11-year prison sentence in the USA in November 2022 for for conspiring to launder tens of millions of dollars from online scams
  • However, posts recently surfaced online that he had been released from prison
  • Legit.ng fact-checked the claim, which has been making the rounds on social media

Washington, D.C., United States - A few days ago, claims surfaced that convicted international fraudster, Ramon Abbas, popularly known as Hushpuppi, had been released from prison.

Legit.ng observed that a lot of social media users have been recirculating the claim.

Hushpuppi hasn't been released from prison

It would be recalled that Hushpuppi was sentenced in November 2022 in Los Angeles to more than 11 years in federal prison.

He was arrested in Dubai, the United Arab Emirates (UAE) in June 2020, and subsequently flown to the US for trial.
