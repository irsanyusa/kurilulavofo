Rough teenage years

The actor's family relocated from Lompoc, California, to Dallas, Texas. At one point, he lived in Georgetown, Texas, and later Cedar Hill, Texas.

Did Jonathan Majors go to Yale?

Yes, the actor is an alumnus of Yale School of Drama, where he graduated with a Master of Fine Arts degree in 2016. Before then, he had studied for his bachelor's degree at the University of North Carolina's School of the Arts.
