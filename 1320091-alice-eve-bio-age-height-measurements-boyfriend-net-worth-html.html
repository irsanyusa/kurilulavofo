Alice Eve husband

Alice Eve and Alex Cowper-Smith had been sweethearts in high school at Westminster School in London. The two met years later and rekindled their romance.

The lovebirds quickly fell in love and later got engaged in August 2014. They had a winter wedding later the same year in December at the Brompton Oratory in London.

According to the star, she was overwhelmed when she saw her husband-to-be at the altar.

When we got to the church, I took a tip from the movies and just looked at my groom. I held eyes with him because I would have been overwhelmed if I had looked at everyone else. I was trying not to cry, but by the time my blurry eyesight got him in full view, I could not help it.
